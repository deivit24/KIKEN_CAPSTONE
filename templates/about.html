About KIKEN [kÄ“ ken]

KIKEN was created to guide DIY investors into the proper asset allocation without the hassel of "Window Shopping" other portfolio models.

The typical work flow for a DIY investor starts by doing research on what to invest in.

Google will give you links of huge investment firms such as, Charles Schwab, Fidelity, Acorns or BlackRock.

These links typically take you to a Risk Tolerance Questionnaire that are designed to get you invested into their products and only gauges on aspect of risk: emotional comfort to financial risk.

There are two other aspects that need to be taken into consideration:

Risk Capacity

This answers the question:

"How much risk am I able to afford to take?"

Risk Required

This answers the question:

"How much risk is required ?"
